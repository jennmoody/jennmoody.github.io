Start Your Spring Adventure

by Josie Moody and Avery Lee

There's nothing better than the great outdoors and no better way to spend your time in nature than hiking the old back trails of the Sawtooths. The feeling of the clean spring air and seeing the gorgeous flowers only enhance the experience.

The trails are good for both beginners and advanced hikers. With lots of trees lining the path, there's plenty of shade to rest in and lots of small streams for the kiddos to play in. Start your spring with a bang and head to the trails to enjoy some awesome weather and even more amazing views.
