Services and Amenities

  • Delicious Complimentary Breakfast

    • Breakfast is served from 5 am to 10 am morning. Enjoy fresh fruit, fluffy waffles, creamy oatmeal and made-to-order omelettes, along with a variety of pasteries, yogurts, and juices in our bright and spacious breakfast room.

  • 24-Hour Fitness Center

    • Cardio and weight training equipment available along with yoga mats, exercise balls and TRX equipment.

  • Heated Indoor Pool with Diving Board

    • Need a place to play while you enjoy your stay? Head down for a swim in our large indoor pool with diving board and children's area.

  • Free High-Speed Wifi

    • Sign-in code available at check-in.

  • Friendly Staff Available 24-Hours

    • Forgot your toothbrush? We have one! Need more towels? Just ask. We are available anytime to assist and serve you.

  • Childcare During Temple Hours

    • We offer a playroom for children along with low-cost, short-term babysitting services for our guests.

  • Family History Center

    • Check out our on-site family history center with staff available to answer questions and assist you with printing ordinance cards.

  • Just need a place to change?

    • Free access to bathrooms and changing facilities available for those traveling to the temple.
