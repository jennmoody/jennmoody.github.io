Meridian Temple

The Meridian Temple is located in the north-west corner of Meridian, Idaho. It has been just over a year since it's dedication and it is as busy as ever. Located at the top of a hill, it boasts stunning views of the valley below. The temple grounds are adorned with many beautiful native plants and trees. The west side of the temple overlooks a beautiful pond. This spot is great photos and peaceful meditation.

The three-story design of the Meridian Temple makes it a prominent figure in the community. It is a beautiful piece of architecture that calls the attention of anyone who passes by. It is blessing to everyone that lives in Meridian, as well as the many communities from the temple district that it serves.
