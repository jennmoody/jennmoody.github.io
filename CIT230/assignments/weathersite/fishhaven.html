View From the Sky

by Avery Lee and Josie Moody

Imagine being lifted off the ground and feeling the wind on your face. You've now reached 1,000 feet in the air. You're feeling terrified, but ultimately relaxed from your lack of control. Hot air ballooning can primarily effect your well-being, making you feel happier and increasing your heart rate even after your ride is over.

Hot air ballooning can give you a higher perspective of your environment, provide peace of mind, and create unique and unforgettable memories. The average height that a hot air balloon flies is 2,000 feet. Some hot air balloons can fit up to 80 passengers, though the average hot air balloon can only fit 4 to 8 people. Hot air ballooning is a great way to set off your spring.
