Fresh Air and Fun

by Avery Lee and Josie Moody

Spring has come, warm weather is on the way, but the mountains seem to be frozen in winter. The temperatures are ranging from 25-39 degrees, perfect weather for sledding or just good old snow ball fights. Its easy to enjoy the beautiful weather. Just head on up the nearest mountain.

For example the Sawtooths, the Walkingtons, and the Pendletons are all great spots to visit. They even have small picnic benches to rest and have some lunch. Kick off your spring with some fresh mountain air.
