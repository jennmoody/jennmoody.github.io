Spring is Blooming

by Josie Moody and Avery Lee

Thanks to the plentiful snowfall and rainfall this year, the flowers are growing in abundance. Their color and fragrance blankets the hills and leaves a pretty picture for all who see them. You can gather them in bundles to take home or you can just linger in them for a bit and enjoy the beautiful weather we're having.

The most abundant meadows are full of mainly Chrysanthimums and Daisies, along with a couple Indian Paintbrushes, sprinkled in. This is the perfect environment for spending time with family and having a picnic.
